TENDER NOTICE — CLEANTECH SME SCALE-UP GRANT [T21]
Tender ID: T21
Language: English
Sector: cleantech
Budget: USD 200,000
Deadline: 2026-07-02
Region: East Africa
Eligibility: SMEs in renewable energy or water purification with 10-100 employees

OVERVIEW
Scale-up funding is available for established clean technology SMEs seeking to expand their renewable energy or water treatment solutions across East Africa. Applicants must provide a detailed market entry plan for at least two new countries and demonstrate existing revenue of at least USD 100,000 per annum.

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
Grantees will be required to open a dedicated project bank account and submit monthly financial reports. The grant cannot be used to repay existing debts or to pay dividends to shareholders. An independent financial audit, at the grantee's expense, is required at project completion. The donor logo must appear on all project communications and outputs.

CONTACT
All inquiries must be submitted in writing via the official grants portal.
No telephone inquiries will be entertained. The granting body reserves
the right to amend these terms at any time prior to the application deadline.
