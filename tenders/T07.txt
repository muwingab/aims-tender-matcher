TENDER NOTICE — AGRITECH MARKET LINKAGE SEED GRANT [T07]
Tender ID: T07
Language: English
Sector: agritech
Budget: USD 5,000
Deadline: 2026-05-18
Region: East Africa
Eligibility: Early-stage agritech startups with fewer than 10 employees

OVERVIEW
Seed funding is available for startups building digital market linkage platforms that connect smallholder farmers directly to buyers, reducing intermediary costs and improving price transparency. Solutions that incorporate real-time price data and SMS-based alerts for low-literacy farmers are particularly encouraged.

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
This is a seed grant for pre-revenue or early-revenue startups. Applicants must not have received more than USD 20,000 in prior grant funding from any source. A pitch deck of no more than 10 slides is required in addition to the standard application form. Shortlisted teams will be invited to a virtual pitch session with the selection panel.

CONTACT
All inquiries must be submitted in writing via the official grants portal.
No telephone inquiries will be entertained. The granting body reserves
the right to amend these terms at any time prior to the application deadline.
