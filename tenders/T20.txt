TENDER NOTICE — HEALTHTECH DIAGNOSTICS GRANT [T20]
Tender ID: T20
Language: English
Sector: healthtech
Budget: USD 200,000
Deadline: 2026-06-17
Region: Sub-Saharan Africa
Eligibility: Health technology companies with CE or WHO-EUL certified products

OVERVIEW
This grant supports the scale-up of point-of-care diagnostic tools for infectious and non-communicable diseases in resource-limited settings. Applicants must demonstrate clear distribution strategies and partnerships with national health systems or accredited healthcare facilities.

APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS
Product certification documentation must be submitted as part of the application package. Applications lacking valid certification evidence will not be reviewed. The grant covers direct project costs only; capital expenditure exceeding 30% of the total budget requires prior approval from the grants committee. Progress reports are due quarterly from the date of the first disbursement.

CONTACT
All inquiries must be submitted in writing via the official grants portal.
No telephone inquiries will be entertained. The granting body reserves
the right to amend these terms at any time prior to the application deadline.
